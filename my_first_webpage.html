Galiano Island

A small island in the Salish Sea

This is a small web page that will list some essential information about Galiano island: places to visit, animals you will see and good restaurants (not many!)

Places to Visit:

  1. Bluffs Park (Bring a picnic!)
  2. Bellhouse Park
  3. Montague Harbour
  4. Bodega Ridge
  5. Pebble Beach
